6 - SCORING AND PRIZES 

6.1 - SCORING.  

6.1.1 - Participants earn "reputation points" by racing in Grand Prix events (and there are nine (9) weekends of events) from the Opening Date up until the Closing Date. The maximum reputation points that a Participant can earn in a single Grand Prix event is 89,000, and in the Tournament as a whole is 801,000. Grand Prix™ events are split into 3 Rounds; the Qualifying Round (comprising 3 x Qualifying Sessions on Wednesday, Thursday and Friday), the Opening Round (on Saturday), and the Final Round (on Sunday). Participants can register for a Grand Prix™ event at any time until 1 hour from the end of the final Qualifying Round. Participants can enter a Qualifying Session or Round at any time during it until 10 minutes from the end of it. The Qualifying Sessions run from 0800 UTC to 0700 UTC the following day, the Opening Round runs from 0800 to 0700 UTC, and the Final Round runs from 0800 to 0800 UTC. In the Final Round, Participants can enhance the Reputation they can earn by finishing in a higher position and competing in higher difficulty tiers. Participants’ reputation points count towards the score of their chosen Formula 1® team, as outlined below. 

6.1.2 - On the Monday following each Grand Prix™ event, we rank the teams according to their Participants’ median reputation scores. We do this is to account for disparities in team numbers. (To find the median, we arrange Participant points in a given team from lowest to highest. If the number of Participants is odd, the median is the middle score in the list. If the number of data points is even, the median is the mean of the middle two scores in the list.)

6.1.3 - Points for the Grand Prix™ events are allocated to teams as follows:

Position - Points

1st - 25

2nd - 18

3rd - 15

4th - 12

5th - 10

6th - 8

7th - 6

8th - 4

9th - 2

10th - 1

6.1.4 - While players can join the Tournament at any time, only reputation points earned after the Opening Date will be tallied towards your personal and nominated Formula 1® team's rankings.

6.1.5 - The team which finishes with the highest reputation points will be crowned the winner ("Winning Team"). The teams with the second and third-highest reputation points (the "Second Place Team" and "Third Place Team" respectively) will also be relevant for the purposes of the Grand Cash Prizes.

6.2 - GRAND CASH PRIZES. 

6.2.1 - The 5 highest-scoring Participants (in reputation points) on the Winning Team will each receive a US$2,500 (two thousand five hundred US dollars) cash prize ("First Place Team Winners").

6.2.2 - The 5 highest-scoring Participants (in reputation points) on the Second Place Team will each receive a US$1,000 (one thousand US dollars) cash prize ("Second Place Team Winners").

6.2.3 - The 5 highest-scoring Participants (in reputation points) on the Third Place Team will each receive a US$500 (five hundred US dollars) cash prize ("Third Place Team Winners").

6.2.4 - Collectively, the fifteen winners referred to in this clause 6.2 will be referred to as "Grand Prize Winners".

6.3 - MONTHLY PRIZES. 

6.3.1 - During the Tournament, at the end of each month, the Participants with the highest reputation scores in that month's leader board ("Monthly Winners"), will win in-game bundles consisting of items and/or virtual currency from the in F1® Clash Game (details of which are set out on the Microsite).
